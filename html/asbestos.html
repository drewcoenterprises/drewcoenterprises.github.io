Asbestos

Asbestos is a tiny fiber found in many old building materials. From tiles on the floor to insulation in the ceiling, asbestos was used everywhere for its strength and durability. Unfortunately, due to its small size, asbestos fibers can be harmful to humans that come into contact with it. Contact with asbestos fibers can cause the lung condition, Mesothelioma, which is fatal.

Abatement / Inspections

Whether residential or commercial, we will have one of our licensed asbestos inspectors visit your site, gather samples of suspect material(s), and provide a complete report.

When you have issues and/or concerns regarding asbestos in your home or business, think of Drewco as your full service contractor for both inspections and abatement.

Safety

Drewco adheres to the safety regulations found under section 1926.1101 of the OSHA safety laws regarding asbestos.


Drewco provides all employees with the propper safety equipment that satisfies the OSHA standards which can be found in section 1910.134.
