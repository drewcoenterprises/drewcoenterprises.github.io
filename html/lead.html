Lead

Lead is a heavy metal that is extremely malleable, which made it useful in many early construction applications. Remnants of lead in construction can be found today in the forms of old lead pipe and, more commonly, lead paint. Due to the highly toxic nature of lead, it is no longer used in construction and should be removed by professionals whenever possible to avoid exposure.

Abatement / Inspections

Drewco is licensed and qualified to remove hazardous lead. Lead takes many forms, such as the peeling lead paint pictured here. If you are concerned about lead in your home or place of business, contact us about our lead inspection and abatement services. Our licensed lead inspectors will visit your site, take samples of the material in question and get back to you with a full report.
